🍬 Sandy Crush — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026 · App package: com.sandycrush.sandy_crush

Sandy Crush ("the Game", "we") is a casual match-3 puzzle game. This policy explains what information the Game handles. The short version: we do not collect, store or share your personal data on any server.

1. Data stored on your device only

Your game progress (levels, stars, coins, gems, lives, settings, achievements) is saved only on your own device using local storage. It never leaves your device, and we cannot access it. Uninstalling the Game deletes this data.

2. In-app purchases

Optional purchases (coin and gem packs) are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details, card numbers or billing address. Purchases are governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

3. Google Sign-In (optional)

If a future version offers "Sign in with Google" and you choose to use it, the Game receives only your basic profile (name, e-mail address and profile picture) to display inside the Game. This information is not transmitted to any server of ours, not stored outside your device, and not shared with anyone. You can sign out at any time from the home screen.

4. No ads, no analytics, no tracking

The Game contains no advertising, no third-party analytics SDKs, no tracking technologies, and builds no advertising profiles.

5. Children

The Game is not directed at children under 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, no personal data is collected from children either.

6. Permissions

The Game requests Internet access solely to enable Google Play Billing and optional Google Sign-In. No other permissions (location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage) are requested.

7. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the Game handles data, this page will be updated and the effective date revised before the change ships.
